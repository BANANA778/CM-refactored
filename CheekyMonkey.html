Welcome to Cheeky Monkey Toys!

We're your local, family-owned toy store offering a joyful collection of high-quality, interactive toys for children of all ages.

Welcome to our expanded Cheeky Monkey Toys!

We are a local, family owned and operated, independent toy store, now with two locations to serve you. We have been in business since 1999. In 2007 we moved from a smaller location to our present location a block from El Camino Real in Menlo Park. In 2022 we expanded to two locations with the purchase of Adventure Toys & Teachers' Supplies, which has been a fixture of Los Altos since 1986.

We offer a wide range of products for children from newborns to adults - anyone young at heart. Knowledgeable staff are always available to help you choose age-appropriate gifts and we offer complimentary gift wrapping. We strive to make your shopping experience a joyful one.
